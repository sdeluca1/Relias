Welcome to the DeLuca Movie Database!

      Enter a title of a movie or any keywords of a movie into the search bar.
      If there is a movie with the searched word or words in the title, they will display.
      If no movie matches the word or words in the title, you will get a message that no results were found.
      If there are a lot of results from a search, the results will be split up into pages. Enter a page number to find the movies on that page.
      You can also find the current trending movies by clicking the Trending button. Clicking Clear Trending will clear out the clutter so you can search more movies.
      Have fun searching all of your favorite movies and finding new movies to watch!
